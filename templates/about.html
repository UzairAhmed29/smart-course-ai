Technical Implementation

A deep dive into the dual-engine architecture of SmartCourse.

TF-IDF Engine

The Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency model serves as our classical recommendation engine. It calculates the statistical importance of words across 3,500+ course descriptions.

Scikit-learn Cosine Similarity N-Grams

Best for: Finding specific tools (e.g., "Tableau", "React") and exact technical phrases.

Neural Engine

Our deep learning engine utilizes Sentence-Transformers to map text into a 384-dimensional dense vector space. This allows the system to understand the context and synonyms of your query.

all-MiniLM-L6-v2 PyTorch Semantic Search

Best for: Broad conceptual queries (e.g., "How to start a business" or "AI for beginners").

Dataset Specifications

The SmartCourse engine is trained on a comprehensive dataset of 3,500+ curated online courses. Every course entry undergoes a rigorous pre-processing pipeline before being indexed by our models.

  • Pre-processing: Lemmatization and stop-word removal using spaCy.
  • Metadata: Includes Course Name, University, Level, and Rating.
  • Scope: Covers 15+ departments from Computer Science to Fine Arts.
